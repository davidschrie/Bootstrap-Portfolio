About Me

Nice to meet you, and thanks for checking out my page. My name is David Schriesheim, and I’m from Chicago, Illinois. My hometown of the Windy City has played a large in shaping who I am today. My favorite food is deep-dish pizza, and my favorite sports teams are the cubs, bears, blackhawks, and bulls. Throughout high-school and the beginning of college I was a competitive swimmer, taking up most of my time. The spare time I had was mainly spent on sleeping, eating, or playing video games. After graduating college with a sociology major and political science minor, I moved to LA, where I’m currently working for Bird, the last-mile electric scooter sharing vehicle transportation company."
